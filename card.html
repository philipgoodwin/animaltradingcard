North American Beaver

In 1975 the beaver was officially named the national animal of Canada.

  • Scientific Name: Castor Canadensis
  • Average Length: 40 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 24 years
  • Habitat: Near lakes, rivers, streams, and ponds

Beavers are herbivores that eat leaves, roots, aquatic plants, and bark.
